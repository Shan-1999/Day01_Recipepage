Oreo Milkshake

"This was the perfect cookies and cream milkshake. The flavors of Oreo always remind me of being a kid,
and a milkshake is a great refreshing summertime sweet. Topping with extra Oreos added crunch and a little indulgence,
which I loved. Make a batch for the whole family or enjoy just yourself!"

Ingredients:

  • 6 chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo®)
  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • ¾ cup milk

Directions:

Blend chocolate sandwich cookies, ice cream, and milk together in a blender until smooth. Pour into 2 glasses.

Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories-249kcal
  • Fat-11g
  • Carbs-33g
  • Protein-6g
